PROTOTYPES
The head office established in Shanghai at 2008 is the first great milestone in the journey we've been through, followed by the rapid growth in recent years, ACTIVATION GROUP extended offices established in Beijing, Hong Kong and Singapore to provide the full coverage of services across the region.

The acquisition of company that shares the same vision has been proven to bring in strong synergy to the group. We kept pushing the industry boundary by delivering one-stop marketing solutions across multiple disciplines. In order to stay ahead today's market's needs and our prestigious clients' expectation, ACTIVATION GROUP successfully acquired leading PR agency, Liquid Element in 2013 and Nodeplus, an award winning digital agency in 2014, to offer fully integrated marketing services in both brick-and-mortar as well as digital platforms.
Diversity & Distinctive Minds
